INSTRUCTIONS

  • This will be a team game with max 2 players.
  • The team who is fast will win the game.
  • Once pressed the submit button, even without choosing an answer, you will not be able to attempt that question again. No points would be deducted for the question not answered.
  • The team first to answer the question will be given privilege (either freeze or skip) to freeze one opponent for 2 mins.During that period, the freezed team will not be able to play.
  • On the basis of the scores at the end of each level, teams will be allowed to enter the next level.
  • Difficulty level increases with each level.
